How it works:
  1. Point your phone camera at the computer screen and record a video while scrolling through the questions. Keep each question steady on screen ~1s. Hold as still as you can and avoid glare — the steadier the shot, the cleaner the read.
  2. Stop recording, come back here, upload the video below.
  3. Frames are scanned on-device, duplicates dropped, and their text is read locally with OCR — then only that text is sent to DeepSeek for answering. No images ever leave your device.
