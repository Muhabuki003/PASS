How it works:
  1. Point your phone camera at the computer screen and record a video while scrolling through the questions. Keep each question steady on screen ~1s. Angled or off-axis shots are fine — the vision model corrects for perspective.
  2. Stop recording, come back here, upload the video below.
  3. Frames are extracted on-device and duplicates dropped, then batched to Google Gemini 2.0 Flash which reads the questions and answers straight from the images. DeepSeek can optionally double-check the answers.
