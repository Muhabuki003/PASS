How it works:
  1. Capture the questions any way you like: record a video while scrolling, snap photos (upload as many as you want), or use the built-in auto-snapper that takes one shot per second. Angled or off-axis shots are fine — the vision model corrects for perspective.
  2. Upload the video/photos below, or tap Auto-snap camera and let it capture for you.
  3. Everything is batched to Google Gemini Flash, which reads the questions and answers straight from the images. DeepSeek can optionally double-check the answers.
